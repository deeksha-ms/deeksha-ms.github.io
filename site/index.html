Welcome!

I am an Electronics and Communication engineer graduated from National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal in 2021. I am deeply interested in biomedical signal processing, machine learning, and human-computer interactions. I envision employing algorithmic approach to develop inexpensive diagnostic and prognostic devices.

In my undergraduate years, I have explored a plethora of fields including but not limited to speech processing, computational imaging, and diagnostic modeling with lung auscultation data. Parallely, I enjoyed working on digital electronics and analog intergrated circuits.

This fall, I will be starting as a doctoral student at the Johns Hopkings University in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. I hope to expand my knowledge of SPML as I work towards practical biomedical applications.
